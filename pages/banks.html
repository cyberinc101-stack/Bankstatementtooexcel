What About Banks Not Listed Here?

If your bank isn't listed, it almost certainly still works. Our AI reads the statement content rather than matching a specific format template. If your bank produces standard text-based PDF statements (which virtually all do), StatementExcel will handle it correctly.

One exception: Scanned or image-based PDFs cannot be processed since they contain images of text rather than actual text. Always use e-statements downloaded from your bank's online portal.

Credit Card Statements

In addition to bank statements, StatementExcel supports credit card statements from all major issuers including American Express, Visa, Mastercard, and Discover. The converter correctly identifies purchases, refunds, interest charges, fees, and payment transactions.
