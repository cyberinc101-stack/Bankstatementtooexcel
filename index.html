How to Convert a Bank Statement PDF to Excel

Converting your bank statement to Excel used to require manual data entry or expensive accounting software. StatementExcel uses AI to automate the process entirely — just upload your PDF, choose your layout, and download a clean spreadsheet.

How it works: Your PDF is processed through our server and the Claude AI API, which reads the statement and extracts every transaction into a structured format — nothing is stored. Choose from 4 Excel layouts and customise which columns appear before downloading as CSV or Excel.

Which Excel Layout Should I Use?

  • Standard — Best for most users. Single amount column with +/− signs, all columns included.
  • Split Columns (Chase style) — Separate Withdrawals and Deposits columns.
  • Accountant View — Absolute values with DR/CR labels. Ideal for bookkeeping and reconciliation.
  • Minimal Clean — Date, description, amount only. Perfect for importing into other tools.
Important: This tool requires text-based PDFs. Scanned or image-based PDFs cannot be parsed. Most modern bank statements downloaded from online banking are text-based and will work correctly.
